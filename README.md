# HR Analytics Dashboard 📊

## Project Overview
This project is an **HR Analytics Dashboard** created using **Power BI** to analyze employee demographics, department performance, salary distribution, and workforce insights.

The dashboard helps organizations understand:
- Gender distribution
- Department-wise employee count
- Salary analysis
- Job satisfaction trends
- Marital status distribution
- Age group filtering

# Key KPIs & Visualizations

## 1. Count of Department by Gender and Department
### Insights:
- Operations department has the highest employee count.
- Male employees are higher than female employees in most departments.
- Finance and HR departments have lower employee counts.

### Visualization Used:
- Clustered Column Chart
  ![Clustered Column Chart](https://github.com/revanthk58/Hr_Analysis-Assignment/blob/main/Screenshots/Screenshot%202026-05-24%20220212.png)

---

## 2. Sum of Hourly Rate by Gender
### Insights:
- Male employees contribute around **61%** of total hourly rate.
- Female employees contribute around **39%**.

- Pie Chart

# Business Insights

- Workforce distribution is heavily concentrated in Operations.
- Salary allocation is highest in Operations and Administration.
- Female employee participation is comparatively lower.
- Job satisfaction varies significantly between departments.
- HR analytics can help management improve employee engagement and retention.
